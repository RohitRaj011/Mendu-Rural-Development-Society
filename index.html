MENDU RURAL DEVELOPMENT SOCIETY is founded by Mr. Mendu Murali in Machilipatnam , Krishna District of ANDHRA PRADESH to help the people by providing basic education and other requirements. The main office of the society is located at Railway Station Road, Vadlamannadu(POST) Gudlavalleru, Krishna(District) of ANDHRA PRADESH.

We are an NGO striving to lift people out of poverty by giving them free education and various skill development programs.We have a Youtube Channel and the donations and earning from the channel help us to continue in our mission.

Aims & Objectives

ॐ सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः सर्वे सन्तु निरामयाः। सर्वे भद्राणि पश्यन्तु मा कश्चिद्दुःखभाग्भवेत। ॐ शान्तिः शान्तिः शान्तिः॥ ॐ सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः -- May all sentient beings be at peace We provide basic necessities such as food & education to every sector of india to bring the smile on each face.

सर्वे सन्तु निरामयाः -- May no one suffer from illness We try to provide medical facilities i.e., hospitals as well as appointing good and professional doctors to give a proper treatment to the economically weaker sections and also encouraging ayurvedic medicines in the rural area .

सर्वे भद्राणि पश्यन्तु मा कश्चिद्दुःखभाग्भवेत -- May all see what is auspicious, may no one suffer We want that every sector of society do not face discrimination in any field. We try to set up orphanage shelters for the poor people who was literally thrown by the society.The society will act in order to prevent child prostitution as well setup a shelter to the children’s those who was affected as well take care of the children to distance themselves from leprosy.

ॐ शान्तिः शान्तिः शान्तिः॥ -- Om peace, peace, peace. At the end , we are trying to reducing the gap between the rich and poor people and to bring happiness and peace in everyone's life, which makes us happy and also enlighten our life.
