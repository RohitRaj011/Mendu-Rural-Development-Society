Free Learning

Because we believe education belongs to each individual

Every child has a different learning style and pace. Each child is unique , not only capable of learning but also capable of succeeding.

In order to provide education and experience to all, we at Mendu Rural Development Society have a Youtube Channel of over 150k subscribers where we provide free education to all specially university students, who are from tier3 colleges and helps them to prepare for exams.
Alongwith classes through youtube channel we also provide opportunities for internships,trainings etc. with certificate and various rewards according to the performance of students.

Our Mission

We are an NGO striving to lift people out of poverty by giving them free education and various skill development programs.

We have a Youtube Channel and the donations and earning from the channel help us to continue in our mission.

At the end, we are trying to reducing the gap between the rich and poor people and to bring happiness and peace in everyone's life, which makes us happy and also enlighten our life.
